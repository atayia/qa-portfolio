About Me & This Portfolio

I’m Marie-Jo Atayi, building my QA brand under Heat QA Solutions. This portfolio highlights my Manual QA skills through structured test cases, test plans, requirements, bug reports, and screenshots.

Designed for recruiters and potential clients, it provides clear access to client-specific deliverables and showcases my approach to validating functionality, UI quality, and thorough documentation. Automation QA projects are coming soon.

Portfolio Overview

This portfolio is organized by unofficial client projects. Each client has a dedicated summary page with clickable deliverables, as well as internal pages that present detailed QA documentation in a clean, readable format.

  • Client Summary Pages — High-level overviews for each unofficial client, with tiles linking to all deliverables.
  • Test Plans — Internal HTML pages outlining objectives, scope, approach, resources, and deliverables.
  • Requirements — Internal HTML pages documenting functional and UI requirements used as the basis for testing.
  • Test Cases — Structured, feature-grouped test cases presented on dedicated internal pages, with screenshots linked where applicable.
  • Bug Reports — Internal pages with expandable bug report sections, designed to grow as additional defects are documented.
  • Screenshots — Dedicated pages displaying thumbnail previews of execution evidence, linking to full-size images.
  • Automation (Coming Soon) — Placeholder sections and folders reserved for future automation QA work.

All clients are unofficial and used strictly for demonstration purposes. Screenshots with sensitive information are censored where applicable.
